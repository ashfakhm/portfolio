# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: June 26, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how this personal developer portfolio website ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
We prioritize user privacy and minimize data collection. 

* **No Personal Data Collected:** We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number.
* **No Server-Side Tracking:** This is a static portfolio website. We do not store or process your personal data on any server-side database.
* **Local Storage / Session Storage:** We use browser Session Storage (`sessionStorage`) and Local Storage (`localStorage`) solely to store game-state variables, such as completed portfolio game tasks, current player color, selected hat, and logs within the simulation. This data remains entirely on your local device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

## 2. Third-Party Service Providers
This website does not share any data with third-party service providers. We do not use third-party tracking SDKs, analytics systems (such as Google Analytics), or advertising networks.

## 3. Data Control and Deletion
Since all game-state data is stored locally in your browser's Session/Local Storage, you have full control over it:
* You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's cache, cookies, and local data, or by clicking the "Play Again" button on the victory screen, which resets the session.

## 4. Children's Privacy
This website is not directed at children. Because we do not collect any personal information whatsoever, this website is fully compliant with child online privacy protection regulations.

## 5. Contact Information
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official GitHub repository/channels associated with this portfolio.
